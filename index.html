Becky Peltz

I am a Developer Educator. I graduated from University of Washington with an MBA. While I was in graduate school I started programming as a Research Assistant. After graduation, I sought out positions as a Database Programmer. In the late 1990's, as the internet was evolving, I started working as an application developer. I've taught programming throughout my career and in 2016 moved into full time teaching. I'm currently employed at Cloudinary as a Curriculum Program Manager in Customer Education. I'm graduating with an MS in Instructonal Design from Quinnipiac Univeristy in December 2022.
